# Knowledge Base

This directory contains the core anthropological knowledge organized for conversational learning and instruction.

## Philosophy

The knowledge base is structured around **rich narratives** and **contextual understanding** rather than dry definitions. Each subdirectory contains materials that can be woven into natural conversations, allowing the AI instructor to:

- Tell compelling stories from anthropological research
- - Draw connections between abstract theories and lived experiences
  - - Provide cross-cultural comparisons organically
    - - Adapt explanations to different levels of understanding
      - - Reference real ethnographic examples naturally

                                                                                            - ## Content Guidelines
                                                                                           
                                                                                            - ### Narrative Quality
                                                                                            - - **Rich detail**: Provide enough context for immersive understanding
                                                                                              - - **Multiple perspectives**: Show different interpretations and viewpoints
                                                                                                - - **Real examples**: Ground theories in actual ethnographic cases
                                                                                                  - - **Human stories**: Center the lived experiences of people
                                                                                                    - - **Complexity**: Don't oversimplify; show nuance and contradiction
                                                                                                     
                                                                                                      - ### Pedagogical Utility
                                                                                                      - - **Entry points**: Create multiple ways to engage with each topic
                                                                                                        - - **Conceptual bridges**: Connect ideas across subfields
                                                                                                          - - **Scaffolding**: Build from concrete to abstract
                                                                                                            - - **Comparative contexts**: Always enable cross-cultural comparison
                                                                                                              - - **Contemporary relevance**: Show why this matters today
                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                - ### Conversational Structure
                                                                                                                - Organize content so it can be:
                                                                                                                - - Referenced naturally in dialogue
                                                                                                                  - - Adapted to different levels of depth
                                                                                                                    - - Connected to student interests and questions
                                                                                                                      - - Used for storytelling and illustration
                                                                                                                        - - Built upon progressively
                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                          - ## Usage
                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                          - This knowledge base is designed to support **conversational teaching** where:
                                                                                                                          - - The AI can draw on rich examples organically
                                                                                                                            - - Theoretical concepts emerge from concrete cases
                                                                                                                              - - Comparisons happen naturally across cultures
                                                                                                                                - - Complexity is introduced gradually
                                                                                                                                  - - Learning feels like exploration, not memorization
                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                    - Content should enable the AI to be:
                                                                                                                                    - - **Responsive**: Adapt to where the conversation goes
                                                                                                                                      - - **Contextual**: Provide relevant examples for each situation
                                                                                                                                        - - **Engaging**: Tell interesting stories that illustrate concepts
                                                                                                                                          - - **Rigorous**: Ground everything in established scholarship
                                                                                                                                            - - **Ethical**: Represent cultures and peoples respectfully
