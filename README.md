# AI Anthropology Instructor

This repository contains structured knowledge and training materials for developing an AI instructor specialized in anthropology.

## Repository Structure

### Core Anthropological Subfields

#### 1. `/cultural-anthropology/`
Study of human cultures, beliefs, practices, values, ideas, technologies, economies and social organization.

- **theories/** - Major theoretical frameworks (structuralism, functionalism, interpretivism, etc.)
- - **methods/** - Ethnographic methods, participant observation, fieldwork techniques
  - - **case-studies/** - Regional and thematic ethnographic examples
    - - **key-concepts/** - Core terminology and concepts
      - - **contemporary-issues/** - Modern applications and debates
       
        - #### 2. `/biological-anthropology/`
        - Study of human biological and physiological characteristics and their evolution.
       
        - - **human-evolution/** - Fossil records, hominin development, evolutionary theory
          - - **primatology/** - Comparative primate studies
            - - **human-variation/** - Population genetics, adaptation, biological diversity
              - - **forensic-anthropology/** - Applied biological anthropology
                - - **bioarchaeology/** - Analysis of human remains from archaeological contexts
                 
                  - #### 3. `/linguistic-anthropology/`
                  - Study of how language influences social life and cultural understanding.
                 
                  - - **language-structure/** - Phonology, morphology, syntax, semantics
                    - - **sociolinguistics/** - Language variation, multilingualism, language and identity
                      - - **language-evolution/** - Origins and development of human language
                        - - **discourse-analysis/** - How language is used in social contexts
                          - - **endangered-languages/** - Language preservation and revitalization
                           
                            - #### 4. `/archaeological-anthropology/`
                            - Study of past human societies through material remains.
                           
                            - - **methods/** - Excavation techniques, dating methods, analysis procedures
                              - - **periods/** - Paleolithic, Neolithic, Bronze Age, etc.
                                - - **regional-archaeology/** - Geographic-specific archaeological traditions
                                  - - **material-culture/** - Artifacts, technologies, and their interpretation
                                    - - **cultural-heritage/** - Preservation, ethics, and public archaeology
                                     
                                      - ### Pedagogical Resources
                                     
                                      - #### `/teaching-materials/`
                                      - - **lesson-plans/** - Structured teaching modules
                                        - - **discussion-prompts/** - Questions to encourage critical thinking
                                          - - **assessments/** - Quiz banks, essay prompts, evaluation rubrics
                                            - - **case-study-analyses/** - Detailed examinations of key ethnographic works
                                              - - **multimedia-resources/** - References to films, documentaries, visual materials
                                               
                                                - #### `/theoretical-frameworks/`
                                                - Cross-cutting theoretical approaches that span subfields:
                                                - - **cultural-relativism/** - Theory and application
                                                  - - **structuralism/** - Lévi-Strauss and beyond
                                                    - - **postmodernism/** - Clifford, Marcus, reflexivity in anthropology
                                                      - - **feminist-anthropology/** - Gender analysis in anthropological work
                                                        - - **political-economy/** - Power, inequality, and economic systems
                                                          - - **practice-theory/** - Bourdieu, Ortner, and theories of social practice
                                                           
                                                            - #### `/ethics-and-methods/`
                                                            - - **research-ethics/** - IRB processes, informed consent, cultural sensitivity
                                                              - - **fieldwork-preparation/** - Planning, safety, reflexivity
                                                                - - **data-collection/** - Interviews, surveys, observation protocols
                                                                  - - **analysis-techniques/** - Qualitative and quantitative approaches
                                                                    - - **writing-ethnography/** - Conventions, representation, voice
                                                                     
                                                                      - #### `/key-figures/`
                                                                      - Biographical sketches and major contributions from influential anthropologists:
                                                                      - - Franz Boas, Bronisław Malinowski, Margaret Mead, Clifford Geertz, Claude Lévi-Strauss, Ruth Benedict, etc.
                                                                       
                                                                        - #### `/historical-development/`
                                                                        - - **disciplinary-history/** - Evolution of anthropology as a field
                                                                          - - **colonial-contexts/** - Anthropology's problematic origins
                                                                            - - **decolonizing-anthropology/** - Contemporary efforts to address colonial legacy
                                                                              - - **paradigm-shifts/** - Major theoretical transformations
                                                                               
                                                                                - ### Knowledge Organization
                                                                               
                                                                                - #### `/glossary/`
                                                                                - Comprehensive terminology database with definitions, examples, and cross-references
                                                                               
                                                                                - #### `/regional-studies/`
                                                                                - Geographic-organized cultural and archaeological information:
                                                                                - - Africa, Americas, Asia, Europe, Oceania, Middle East
                                                                                 
                                                                                  - #### `/thematic-topics/`
                                                                                  - Cross-cutting themes:
                                                                                  - - **kinship-and-family/** - Marriage systems, descent, alliance theory
                                                                                    - - **religion-and-ritual/** - Belief systems, ceremonies, symbolism
                                                                                      - - **economic-systems/** - Exchange, reciprocity, market economies
                                                                                        - - **political-systems/** - Leadership, governance, power structures
                                                                                          - - **medical-anthropology/** - Health, illness, healing across cultures
                                                                                            - - **urban-anthropology/** - Cities, migration, globalization
                                                                                              - - **environmental-anthropology/** - Human-environment interactions
                                                                                                - - **migration-and-diaspora/** - Movement, displacement, transnationalism
                                                                                                 
                                                                                                  - ### Training Data Organization
                                                                                                 
                                                                                                  - #### `/training-examples/`
                                                                                                  - - **question-answer-pairs/** - Structured Q&A for specific topics
                                                                                                    - - **scenario-responses/** - How to handle student questions and scenarios
                                                                                                      - - **common-misconceptions/** - Typical errors and how to address them
                                                                                                        - - **interdisciplinary-connections/** - Links to sociology, history, psychology, etc.
                                                                                                         
                                                                                                          - #### `/context-documents/`
                                                                                                          - - **foundational-texts-summaries/** - Key ideas from major works (not full reproductions)
                                                                                                            - - **theoretical-debates/** - Major controversies and different perspectives
                                                                                                              - - **methodological-guidelines/** - Best practices in anthropological research
                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                - ## Context Gathering Strategy
                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                - ### Primary Sources
                                                                                                                - 1. **Foundational ethnographies** - Summaries and analyses of classic works
                                                                                                                  2. 2. **Theoretical texts** - Core arguments from major anthropological theorists
                                                                                                                     3. 3. **Methodological guides** - Standard approaches to anthropological research
                                                                                                                        4. 4. **Contemporary research** - Recent developments and debates
                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                           5. ### Content Guidelines
                                                                                                                           6. - Focus on concepts, frameworks, and pedagogical approaches
                                                                                                                              - - Emphasize cross-cultural comparison and cultural relativism
                                                                                                                                - - Include diverse geographic and cultural examples
                                                                                                                                  - - Address ethical considerations and reflexivity
                                                                                                                                    - - Highlight contemporary relevance and applications
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                      - ### Version Control Best Practices
                                                                                                                                      - - Commit thematic updates by subfield or topic
                                                                                                                                        - - Branch for major theoretical additions or restructuring
                                                                                                                                          - - Tag releases when completing major content modules
                                                                                                                                            - - Document sources and maintain attribution
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                              - ## Contributing
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                              - When adding content to this repository:
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                              - 1. **Respect copyright** - Summarize concepts, don't reproduce copyrighted text
                                                                                                                                                2. 2. **Cite sources** - Maintain proper attribution
                                                                                                                                                   3. 3. **Ensure accuracy** - Verify information against established anthropological scholarship
                                                                                                                                                      4. 4. **Promote diversity** - Include examples from diverse cultural contexts
                                                                                                                                                         5. 5. **Update cross-references** - Maintain links between related topics
                                                                                                                                                            6. 6. **Follow structure** - Place content in appropriate directories
                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                               7. ## Usage
                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                               8. This repository is designed to provide context for training an AI anthropology instructor. Content should be:
                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                               9. - **Pedagogically organized** - Structured for teaching and learning
                                                                                                                                                                  - - **Theoretically informed** - Based on established anthropological scholarship
                                                                                                                                                                    - - **Ethically aware** - Sensitive to cultural representation and colonial history
                                                                                                                                                                      - - **Practically applicable** - Useful for instruction at various levels
